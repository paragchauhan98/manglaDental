About Us

Your Smile, Our Passion

At Mangle Dental Hospital, dentistry is built on trust, care, and a passion for creating healthy smiles. Guided by the motto “Your Smile, Our Passion,” Dr. Parag Chauhan brings years of expertise and a gentle approach to every patient who walks through the door.

Unlike large clinics, our hospital is centered around personalized care. Every treatment plan is carefully tailored to individual needs—whether it’s preventive care, cosmetic enhancements, or restorative solutions. By combining advanced technology with a compassionate touch, we ensure that each visit is as comfortable as it is effective.


We believe that a beautiful smile can transform confidence and well-being. At Mangle Dental Hospital, you’re not just receiving dental treatment—you’re joining a space where your comfort and long-term oral health truly matter.
